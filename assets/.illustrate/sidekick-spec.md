# Sidekick — Character Source of Truth

> Sidekick is the **yellow instance** of the **Sidekick** base model. For
> species-level proportions and the rules on what may vary between sidekicks
> (height, weight, build), see **`.illustrate/sidekick-spec.md`**.

The canonical reference is **`.illustrate/refs/sidekick-character-sheet.png`** (turnaround +
expressions + poses + details + color palette). Always attach it. For a known-good
render to anchor color/proportions/finish, also attach **`.illustrate/refs/sidekick-anchor.png`**
(an approved on-model vignette). Every generation must keep the LOCKED features below
identical and only change the VARIABLE ones.

## LOCKED — never change these

- **Species / vibe:** "Gloob" — cheerful, optimistic, friendly, cute.
- **Material:** smooth glossy soft-vinyl designer toy; subsurface scattering; soft, broad
  specular highlights; soft studio lighting. Polished but NOT wet-looking or over-glossy.
- **Base color (Sidekick):** "Sunny Yellow" = a warm, slightly muted golden-amber yellow,
  ≈ `#EBA63C`. Lighter warm-yellow highlights, soft orange-tinted shadows.
  NOT bright lemon / canary / neon yellow. NOT high-saturation. Keep it warm and muted.
- **Head:** big, round, a touch wider than tall; ~45% of total height.
- **Ears:** two smooth rounded bear-like ears at the upper corners of the head, angled
  slightly outward.
- **Head bumps:** a small crest of ~5 rounded bumps in a row along the top-center of the
  head, between the ears. Always present.
- **Eyes:** two SIMPLE flat jet-black (`#141414`) almond/oval eyes, medium size, set fairly
  wide on the lower-middle of the face, each with only a tiny pinpoint highlight. NOT large,
  NOT sparkly, NO white eye-whites, NO big cartoon shine.
- **Mouth:** one wide, open, happy smile (soft rounded-rectangle), low-center on the face;
  soft red-pink (`#C0413A`) interior; exactly **four** rounded pearl-white teeth (two
  prominent upper, two smaller lower). This is the signature "big smile."
- **Body:** chunky, rounded, short and stout. Short rounded arms ending in **large,
  oversized round mitten-paws** (deliberately big relative to the body), each with exactly
  **four** short rounded nub fingers. Stubby short legs. Small rounded tail.
- **Feet:** glossy pearl-white (`#F5F4ED`) rounded toe-beads (≈3 per foot) at the tips.
- **Proportions:** squat toddler-like; head ≈ 45% of height; short limbs. Keep it chunky.

## VARIABLE — may change per request

- **Body color:** ONLY for named friend variants (e.g. teal Pip, coral Rosa). Default Sidekick is
  always Sunny Yellow. When recoloring, keep the SAME muted saturation level as the yellow —
  no neon, no high-gloss candy.
- **Expression:** one of the canonical set — happy, excited, curious, playful (wink + tongue),
  surprised — built from the SAME eye/mouth construction.
- **Pose:** wave, jump, point, sit, think, cheer, or new poses — proportions + all locked
  features stay identical.
- **Clothing / accessories / held props:** hats, scarves, a skateboard, a book, etc. Additive
  only; must not alter the body.
- **Small unique traits (friends only):** a leaf sprout, blush cheeks, freckles, a horn-bump,
  floppy ears, etc. Small additions; must NOT change the core eyes/mouth or proportions.
- **Scene / background:** any, or none (transparent / flat white).

## DO-NOT (common drift to avoid)

- Do NOT brighten or oversaturate the yellow (no lemon/canary/neon).
- Do NOT enlarge the eyes, add white eye-whites, or big sparkly cartoon highlights.
- Do NOT change the mouth construction or the number/shape of teeth.
- Do NOT slim, stretch, or round-up the body away from its chunky squat build.
- Do NOT change ear shape or drop the head-bump crest.
- Do NOT add/lose fingers or toe-beads, or change paw shape.
